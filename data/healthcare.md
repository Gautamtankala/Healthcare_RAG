Diabetes is a chronic disease that affects blood sugar levels.

Symptoms:
- Frequent urination
- Increased thirst
- Fatigue
- Blurred vision

Treatment:
- Exercise
- Healthy diet
- Medication

Hypertension is also called high blood pressure.

Symptoms:
- Headache
- Dizziness
- Chest pain

Treatment:
- Exercise
- Reduce salt intake
- Medication
